About Us

We are a movement that aims to motivate, empower, and help young people discover their dreams, develop their talents, and build a better future for themselves and their communities. We believe that every young person can be successful. Through our programs, we allow young people to learn, realize themselves, and develop themselves using life skills, economic training, and social and moral education.

Our Vision

To see a generation of motivated, prepared, and successful young people who serve their communities with energy and knowledge.

Our Mission

Creating an environment where young people can discover themselves, develop themselves, and achieve their dreams through education, life skills, and entrepreneurship.

Core Values

  • Trust: We respect and foster trust in our relationships with youth, communities, and our partners.
  • Commitment: We believe that success comes from individual efforts and collective cooperation.
  • Creativity: We encourage young people to use their talents and be creative in solving their own and society's challenges.
  • Values: We provide training based on values that ensure young people become good leaders and a good example for society.
  • Constant Learning: We encourage the spirit of learning and continuous development every day in our youth.
